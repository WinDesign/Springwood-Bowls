Springwood Bowls is part of the Springwood Sports Club and is located in the heart of the beautiful Blue Mountains. We offer an active program of bowls for both men and women throughout the year and warmly welcome new players. Our club is a great place to participate in social, barefoot or competition bowls.

ABOUT CLUB

ABOUT SPRINGWOOD BOWLS

Springwood Bowls has a long and successful history. Officially opened in 1938 it has provided both social and recreation facilities to the community for more than 75 years. Now part of the Springwood Sporting Club it offers a friendly environment for playing bowls for the whole family and a host of facilities within the new modern building. 


The Bowling Club’s two greens are busy throughout the week and often host visiting bowlers for social and tournament play. Our Men’s and Women’s Bowling Clubs welcome visitors and new members. We provide instruction if you wish to take up the great game of bowls. Both men and women’s teams play social bowls every week and enter teams in Zone Pennant events.


We also host Barefoot Bowls for private and corporate events which is a fun way to socialise and a good introduction to the game.

BOWLS A GAME FOR LIFE

BOWLS - IT'S A GAME FOR LIFE

If you are not playing lawn bowls, you don’t know what you’re missing – challenges, light exercise, a low cost sport and above all fun and friendship. If you’re looking for competition or just social bowls, all your needs are catered for at Springwood Bowls. New bowlers are nurtured, coached and encouraged while, for experienced bowlers, there are plenty of challenges. Come on give it a go – it’s a game for life.

It’s never too late to start- you’re never too young or too old. We would love to see here at Springwood bowls, you would be most welcome. It’s easier to start than you think!

BAREFOOT BOWLING

BAREFOOT BOWLING

Experience the best game of barefoot bowls in your life at Springwood Bowls. It’s a game, it’s a great day or night of fun - where you may not only end the night barefoot but you start the event barefoot. 

Barefoot Bowls is the ideal way for you and your friends to spend a relaxing, budget friendly, fun day in the sun, or an evening under the stars. We offer barefoot bowls at a cost of $10 per head with bowling green access, use of club bowls and tuition.

For a booking contact the club on 47511298.

OUR BOWLS PROGRAM

OUR BOWLING PROGRAM

Springwood Bowling Club offers a wide ranging program of bowls for both men women that suits all levels of participation, skills and expectations; there’s something for everybody. The days and times for our recurring events are fairly well but can be subject to change. For full details of our bowling events link to our Bowling Program Document or, you can contact us for more information; use the Contact Us page.


A summary of our bowling program follows:


MEN'S SOCIAL BOWLS - Weekly on Wednesdays, Fridays and Saturdays commencing at 1 pm. Twilight bowls during the summer months at 5 pm and, on the 1st Tuesday of the month mixed pairs/triples at 9.00 am.


WOMEN'S SOCIAL BOWLS - Weekly on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays commencing at 9.00 am.  


MEN'S CHAMPIONSHIP PROGRAM - held from about December through to August each year with varying classification and game formats.


SPECIAL EVENTS - held throughout the year and includes the Jack & Ruby Russel Shield, Anzac Shield, Birney Shield the Springwood in Springtime competition.


PENNANT BOWLS - for members wishing to compete against other clubs. Springwood Bowls is a very competitive club in the Zone 5 Pennant Men's Competition.


Irrespective of which type of bowling activity you may wish to participate in the overall objective of our club is to ensure that members enjoy their bowling.


WOMEN'S BOWLS

WOMEN'S BOWLS

The Springwood Women’s Bowling Club is a progressive and exciting club.


Springwood Bowls has supported women's bowling for a very long time, and we've produced some outstanding female talent over the years. Our members range from beginners all the way up to State qualifying players. New members are always welcomed and will receive all the tutoring and encouragement they need to get the most from this wonderful game.


Our women's bowling club is a great opportunity to socialise while you develop your skills on the green. We play every Tuesday,Thursday and Saturday at the Springwood Sports and Recreation Club. 


Annual Membership is required and gives you membership to the NSW Women’s Bowling Association.


There is plenty of parking and a convenient bus stop.


Our club consists of women of all ages who have one thing in common; we Love Lawn Bowls! Come and join us, make some friends and find out why!


If you want to more information about Springwood Bowls proud women bowlers use the Contact Us page. Alternatively, you can contact the club via email at springwood2777bowls@gmail.com.
